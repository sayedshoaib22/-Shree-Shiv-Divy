About This Service

A complete range of astrology guidance, under one roof

Every astrology consultation starts with your exact birth details — date, time and place. These three pieces of information create your unique birth chart (kundli), which is as individual as your fingerprint.

We read your chart in front of you and explain what it actually shows in plain language. Whether the question is marriage timing, a career move, health concerns, financial decisions or family matters, the session stays focused and practical — no vague predictions, just clear answers you can act on.

A consultation isn't about having your fortune 'told.' It's about understanding what planetary periods you're moving through, where your strengths lie, and what timing is genuinely favourable for major decisions. Follow-up is always welcome if you have questions after the session.
