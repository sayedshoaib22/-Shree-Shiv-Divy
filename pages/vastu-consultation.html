About This Service

Vastu guidance that respects your actual space

Every Vastu consultation begins with understanding your property's layout — entrance direction, room placement, and how the space is currently used.

We prioritise corrections that are simple to apply: furniture placement, colour, and minor adjustments, before ever suggesting structural change. Where a bigger correction is genuinely needed, we explain exactly why.

Vastu works best when it aligns with how you actually live. A technically perfect setup that feels uncomfortable won't serve you. Our approach respects both Vastu principles and the real, lived experience of your space — finding solutions that work for your family or business right now.

In Detail

Areas We Review In Your Space

Main Entrance & Direction

The entry point sets the energy tone for the entire space. We review entrance direction, threshold placement, and practical ways to optimise it without major renovation — sometimes just a small adjustment makes a difference.

Kitchen & Cooking Area

Stove position, sink placement, and cooking direction all matter. We review the kitchen's fire element positioning and suggest adjustments that respect both Vastu and functional cooking flow.

Bedroom & Sleep Direction

Where you sleep and which direction your head points affects rest quality and relationship health. We provide personalised sleeping direction based on your birth details alongside Vastu principles.

Pooja Room or Prayer Area

If your home has a temple or prayer space, its placement, facing direction, and what's stored nearby all carry weight. We ensure it's positioned to support spiritual practice.

Office, Desk & Work Zone

For office spaces, we review owner/manager cabin placement, desk facing direction, and overall workspace layout — tailored to the business type and decision-maker's birth chart.

Water Features & Storage

Placement of water sources (wells, taps, overhead tanks) and storage areas affects the property's energy flow. We review these against directional principles and suggest practical repositioning where needed.
