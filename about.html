About Us

Guided by authentic Vedic ritual, delivered with care.

Shree Shiv Divy is a trusted platform dedicated to providing authentic Vedic Puja Services and Hindu religious ceremonies with devotion, transparency and traditional values. Our mission is to connect every family with experienced, Gurukul-trained Pandits who perform each ritual strictly according to sacred Vedic scriptures and Sanatan Dharma.

Whether you need a Pandit for Puja at home, office, temple or any religious event, our priests are committed to making the occasion spiritually meaningful. We offer offline Pandit services across India and Nepal, while devotees abroad can rely on convenient Online Puja Booking for Havan, astrology consultation, Sankalp Puja and other Vedic rituals from anywhere in the world.

Devotion first: Every Hindu Priest Service is performed with sincerity, mantra accuracy and a focus on the highest benefit for you.
Trust assured: Transparent pricing, authentic process and total confidentiality for every ceremony.
Service every step: From planning to follow-up, our team supports your ritual journey end to end, online or offline.
