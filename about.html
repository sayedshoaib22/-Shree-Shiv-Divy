Our Story

A journey rooted in devotion, built on trust.

What began as one family's commitment to preserving authentic Vedic tradition has grown into a spiritual home for thousands of devotees. Every milestone along the way has been guided by the same values we started with — faith, precision and care.

1

Founded

Shree Shiv Divy began with a single mission — to bring genuine, scripture-accurate Vedic rituals to families who could no longer find them easily.

2

Mission

We set out to make authentic puja and astrology guidance accessible, transparent and deeply personal for every household we serve.

3

Growth

Our circle of Gurukul-trained Pandits expanded to serve devotees across India, Nepal and worldwide through in-person and online consultations.

4

Today

Today, Shree Shiv Divy is a trusted name for Vedic puja and astrology — carrying tradition forward with the same devotion we began with.
